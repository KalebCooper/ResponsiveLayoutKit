# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short: be respectful, be welcoming, assume good faith, and keep discussion focused on the work. Harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory behavior are not tolerated.

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainer at thekalebcooper@gmail.com. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
